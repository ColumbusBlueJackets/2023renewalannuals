NEW for the 2023-24 season, in addition to the Annual Plan (single season renewal) renewal option, we are excited to offer an exciting new Multi-Year Plan as a renewal option! This option removes the hassle of having to renew your tickets each season and allows you to save money by signing up today for the next three seasons.

When taking advantage of the Multi-Year Plan option, you will enjoy the following:

  • Minimum 3% savings off the Annual Plan each season
  • Maximum 5% season-over-season increase on your season tickets each season
